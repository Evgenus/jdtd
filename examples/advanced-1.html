Advanced features: Validation
-----------------------------

With `jdtd` editor you can't produce data with invalid structure. 
However in places where you will enter abstract textual data, you are able to make a mistake.
For such places (input fields) exists validation notification.
When you trying to enter invalid data for field, it receives class `invalid-input`.
You can provide style for it as was made in this example.

Also at this example you can experiment with atomic fields.
Note that empty string is not present in gathered data.
